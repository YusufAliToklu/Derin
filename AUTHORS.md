## Project Description

DERIN (Deep Learning Enhanced Responsive Intelligence Node) is a Digital Individual AI system designed for the NVIDIA Jetson AGX Thor 128GB platform.

## Vision

To create a self-evolving AI companion that:
- Develops its own personality through experience
- Makes ethical decisions based on its own values
- Grows with its user over months and years
- Represents a new paradigm in human-AI relationships

## Key Innovations

1. **70B Supervisor Architecture** - Central "Ana Abi" (Big Brother) that oversees all responses
2. **Night Optimization System** - 70B + Devstral 24B collaborative self-improvement
3. **Experiential Personality Evolution** - No scripts, pure learning
4. **Ethical Autonomy** - Beyond Asimov's 3 Robot Laws
5. **Multi-modal Person Recognition** - Face + voice + gait + behavior

## Academic Goals

- 1-year longitudinal study of AI personality evolution
- Publication in top-tier venues (CHI, HRI, NeurIPS)
- Open-source contribution to Edge AI community
